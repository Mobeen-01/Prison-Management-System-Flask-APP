About Us

Punjab Prisons is a progressive department aiming at the reform and rehabilitation of offenders. We focus on moral education and reintegration programs, ensuring the well-being of inmates during and after their time in prison. Our mission is to transform lives through holistic measures, ultimately contributing to a safer and more inclusive society.

Our Services

Punjab Prisons provides a variety of rehabilitation and reintegration programs for inmates, including vocational training, mental health support, and spiritual guidance. Our services are designed to help inmates successfully re-enter society and lead productive, crime-free lives.
